Reasons nets should be banned

1. Fish are cruely fished up in nets onto docks of boats and are left to suffocate, and die a painful death.
2. Other animals that aren't fish are fished up in the nets, such as dolphins, and they drown.
3. They also are really cruel to animals that get fished up, such as kicking them in the face and dumping them off the boat.
4. They also catch endangered fish, which are slowly dying becuase they can't reporduce faster than ships catch them.
5. Tying back to number 4, this also damages the ecosystem and can affect the rate of reproduction.

But wait! Fishing provides a lot of food for the world.

While this is true, fishing catches lots of endagered species, and people can't eat the fish that insdustrial ships catch with nets, leading to unused fish being stored, or sometimes thrown away.
